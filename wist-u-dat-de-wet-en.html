📚 Did You Know? The Law is Clear!

Legal Framework: VZW Legislation in Practice

Understand your rights as a member of a Non-Profit Association

💡 What Does the Belgian Law Say?

Many people think that a board of a non-profit association can decide everything on its own. However, Belgian law says something different: the members are in charge. The board has the task of managing the community's money, but members have the right to oversee and have a say.

📋 Rights and Duties Overview

Category Member Rights (General Assembly) Board Duties
💰 Finances Right to access all invoices, receipts and accounting records (Art. 3:103 WVV). Obligation to maintain transparent and complete accounting (Art. 3:47 WVV).
⚖️ Decision Making The General Assembly (GA) is the highest body and decides on appointments and bylaws. The board must execute decisions from the GA and cannot act outside the bylaws.
🔍 Oversight 1/5th of members can force an Extraordinary GA (Art. 9:14 WVV). The board must convene the GA within 21 days of a valid request from 1/5th of members.
👥 Membership Right to register as a member after payment of contribution. The board is legally required to maintain an up-to-date member register (Art. 9:3 WVV).
📊 Accountability Right to annual financial statements and approval (discharge) of the board. The board must report annually to members on policy and finances.

🎯 Key Points

  • ✓ Members are sovereign: In a non-profit association, members hold the authority, not the board.
  • ✓ Transparency is mandatory: The board must fully disclose financial information.
  • ✓ Democracy works: 1/5th of members can force an extraordinary meeting.
  • ✓ Good governance is required: The board must report annually to members.
  • ✓ Your vote counts: Each member has a say in the General Assembly.

⚠️ Attention: Things That Are NOT Allowed

According to law, boards are NOT allowed to:

  • ❌ Refuse access to financial documents
  • ❌ Avoid or delay the General Assembly
  • ❌ Remove members from the register without valid reason
  • ❌ Ignore requests for information
  • ❌ Spend money without member approval
