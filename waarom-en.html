🚀 Why? - The Journey to Transparency

Discover why transparency and good governance are essential for our community, and how we take steps together towards trust and accountability.

The Journey to Transparency

Step 1: Listening & Understanding

Members ask questions about how money is used and how the mosque is managed. This is healthy and normal. Asking questions helps the community grow stronger.

What's needed: Open ears, patience, and willingness to listen to everyone's concerns.

Step 2: Dialogue

We have conversations in WhatsApp groups and organize coffee meetings with the board. This shows everyone can speak and ask questions.

What's needed: Communication channels, regular meetings, and an active listening board.

Step 3: Check Legal Basis

We read our VZW statutes (rules). These are articles that define how we can operate. We verify everything complies with the law.

What's needed: Knowledge of VZW law and legal insight.

Step 4: Formal Procedures

Members can request an Extraordinary General Assembly when needed. This is the formal way to discuss important matters democratically.

What's needed: 1 in 5 member signatures (minimum 20% of membership).

Step 5: Make Decisions Together

At the assembly, all members vote on important matters. This happens fairly and anonymously. This is how we shape our future together.

Result: A mosque with full transparency and trust from all members.

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Is this fitna (discord)?

Answer: No! Transparency is not discord. Asking questions and being honest is an Islamic duty. Discord arises when we are dishonest and hide things. Clarity and trust prevent discord.

🔐 Is voting anonymous?

Answer: Yes! We use voting ballots so no one can see how you voted. This gives everyone safety. You can vote what you truly believe, without pressure or fear.

💰 What happens to donations?

Answer: All donations go to the mosque. We use the money for: building, electricity, classes, activities, and care for members in need. Everything is recorded and can be found in the annual report.

👥 Can all members vote?

Answer: Yes! All members have voting rights. This is in the statutes. Your vote is as important as anyone else's. This is called 'Shura' - consultation.

📑 How can I access documents?

Answer: If you want to know more, you can download the statutes. You can also contact the board. All members have the right to information.
